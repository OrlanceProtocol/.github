# üåø Orlance

**Sustainable Yield. Principal Protection. Automated Management.**

Welcome to the official GitHub of **Orlance Protocol**, a fixed-income and yield protocol built natively on the **Arbitrum** ecosystem. 

Orlance focuses on delivering sustainable yield while providing absolute protection for the users' initial capital (principal). By combining Arbitrum's cost efficiency with smart automation features, Orlance offers a secure, fair, and profitable *set-and-forget* investment strategy.

## ‚ú® Key Features

* **Zero Principal Drain**: The initial capital (principal) is never touched. All protocol fees (10% Yield Performance Fee) are strictly deducted from the successfully generated yield or profit.
* **Auto-Roller (Set-and-Forget)**: An automated smart contract system that manages, extends, and compounds profitable positions in the background, with a minimal 1% convenience fee (taken *only* from profits).
* **Pro-Liquidity Provider (LP)**: We allocate the lion's share of trading fees (0.25% out of the 0.3% total) directly to Liquidity Providers to ensure a healthy and deep market liquidity.
